South Coast Customs 4th Meet-up!

SATURDAY 22 JUNE 2024 FROM 19:00-21:00

Join South Coast Customs for their 4th meet-up at Lysses Path, Fareham PO16 7BE, where car enthusiasts gather to showcase their prized vehicles and share their passion for all things automotive. This event promises a vibrant atmosphere with stunning cars and a shared love for motoring culture. We at CityShifts are proud to support South Coast Customs in this exciting gathering, fostering a united and dynamic car community. Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to connect with fellow car lovers and enjoy a day filled with automotive excellence!

Smaller Car Communities

Here at CityShifts, we strive to team up with smaller car communities like ours to grow and exchange services, enriching the car culture together. Our goal is to stand out as a unique car group, organizing amazing events similar to the Gumball 3000 but at an affordable price. We are passionate about collaborating with other groups who share our vision of expanding the car community safely and, most importantly, making it more fun for everyone involved.
